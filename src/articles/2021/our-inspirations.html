Our inspirations

Written by Vincent

What we love in video games

The two games we love the most are Celeste by Extremely Ok Game, Hollow Knight by Team Cherry and today I will explain why.

We both played these games, and even completed a Celeste save file together in a week.
Music is surely what we love the most in video games. When I spend time with Pierre, we ALWAYS have game soundtracks playing in the background.
The soundtracks of Celeste by Lena Rain and Hollow Knight by Christopher Larkin are absolutely stunning and represent a big part of our appreciation of these games.

Visuals are the second thing that made us fall in love with these two games.
The pixel arts of Celeste are so beautiful and are a reason why we use pixel art. And even if we know the game by heart, we are still amazed by Hollow Knight's backgrounds.

Gameplay is also pretty important to us, but as it is sometime impossible to compare two very different gameplays, we tend to compare them very rarely.



What we want to create

We surely don't want to copy the games we love. Our goal is to create the perfect game for us.

I think what we want the most with our games is to tell stories, because there is not such satisfaction as making someone feel something.
Also, Pierre is really excited about composing music for our games (and he is really good at it) so I can want to listen to his stunning soundtracks.

In terms of gameplay, at the moment I am writting this, we are thinking like this :
As we do not have a game idea that we both specificaly want to transform into a real game, we will try a lot of things (by taking part in Game Jams for example) until we create a prototype we think is interesting.
