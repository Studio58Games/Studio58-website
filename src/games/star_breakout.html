  • A space shooter mixed with bullet hell

    Avoid corn to flee as far as possible in this retro endless auto-scroller game and try to beat your high score!
    Made for a 24h Game Jam, this game lets you come aboard a spaceship and drive it across space to avoid corn ears flying all around you.

    When you get overwhelmed by corn, you can shoot with your chicken launcher to have them eat the corn. But use your eggs wisely, you don't have many.
    However, you may come across space stations to refill your egg containers, but beware of evil corns!
